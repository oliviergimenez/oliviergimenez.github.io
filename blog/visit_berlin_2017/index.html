Visit to the IZW Leibniz Institute, Berlin

Mar 8, 2017
I spent 3 days in Berlin at the [Leibniz Institute for Zoo and Wildlife Research](http://www.leibniz-izw.de/welcome.html) catching up with friends and colleagues (L. Marescot, S. Benhaim, S. Kramer-Schadt, A. Courtiol, H. Hofer).

We talked about spotted hyenas and monitoring of large carnivores. I also gave a workshop on inferring occupancy using HMM. I even got in touch with an old acquaintance - Steve Beissinger - who happens to be in Berlin for the year. Steve will come and visit us in Montpellier for a few days in May.
